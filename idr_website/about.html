About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to closing the talent gap in digital, cyber, and technology risk roles. We prepare professionals to thrive in regulated, high-consequence environments where security and compliance are paramount.

Through our exclusive UK university partnerships, we bring rigorous academic insight into real-world, applicable projects. Our approach ensures that every practitioner we train is ready to deploy and instantly add value to your organisation's risk posture.

Advancing the future of digital risk through elite training, innovation, and deployment.
